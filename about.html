About Me

I am a mechanical designer with a decade of experience in design, modeling and detailing of machine components and their respective manufacturing methods (non-serial parts). I can offer the matematical models or the time as a dsigner for the following tasks:

  • • Modeling, detailing and analysis in CAD/CAE software: Solid Works, Fusion 360, Creo Parametric, Top Solid, Siemens NX
  • • Prototyping
  • • Targeted modeling and parameter setting for 3D printing on polymers (FDM)

All the designs focus on processes relying in features from industry 4.0, zero setup and use of improvement and fault identification tools such as PDCA, 5W2H and DMAIC, in addition to making KPI's directed to support FMEA and Design Review
